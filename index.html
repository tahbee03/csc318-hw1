Why I Love Music

Music is a ubiquitous element and it influences each and every one of us in different ways. It is an important part of my life because the messages behind each song I listen to are very sentimental and sometimes even relate to what I go through in my own life. It's also important because different types of music take me through different types of experiences simply because of how they sound -- whether there are lyrics involved or not.
